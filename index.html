mid-2022 The Port of Kijing operates as an Export Import Gateway in Kalimantan

Kijing International Port, West Kalimantan

Kijing Harbour - PT Pelabuhan Indonesia II (Pelindo II) or IPC is completing the construction of the Kijing Port in Mempawah, West Kalimantan. This port will have a dock with a length of 5 km with a port depth of about 15 meters. As one of the hub ports, the Kijing Terminal will later become the main gate for the export / import of goods to and from Kalimantan.

Agricultural IoT technology and processing of agricultural products in modern factories

Internet of Things (IoT)

The application of the Internet of Things (IoT) in agriculture can be in the form of sensor technology for water use, sensors to detect pest attacks, and sensors that know environmental emissions. With this application, agricultural yields can increase rapidly and accurately.
