Charcoal Briquettes Shisha

Coconut charcoal briquettes are charcoal blocks made from crushed coconut shells and printed using natural binders. Briquettes are a necessity for shisha pipe cigarettes. Coconut shell charcoal briquettes produce greater heat and evenly so that the burning time is longer than coal briquettes and the ignition is easier. In addition, coconut charcoal briquettes are safer and more environmentally friendly (they donâ€™t cause smoke). We offer briquettes in various sizes according to customer requests. The briquettes we offer are made 100% with natural ingredients. We guarantee cleanliness and safety from picking coconut to delivery of briquettes to customers
