Jokowi's Mainstay Agricultural Export Commodities: Porang to Swallow's Nest

  • Ahmad Naufal
  • Thursday 19/08/2021
  • 12 Comments

Three agricultural commodities, namely porang, edamame, and swallow's nest, were mentioned by President Jokowi as the mainstay of exports. He conveyed this because agriculture continued to grow positively, even when national economic growth was still negative in the recession zone.

Data from the Central Statistics Agency (BPS) recorded growth in the agricultural sector in the first quarter of 2021 at 2.95 percent, when the economy was still contracting 0.74 percent. Likewise, when the economy is positive and comes out of the recession zone in the second quarter of 2021, the agricultural sector will continue to grow by 0.38 percent.

"I am sure that in the third quarter, agriculture can still grow even better," Jokowi said at the 2021 Inflation Control National Coordination Meeting, Wednesday (25/8).

Jokowi's belief is based on the large market for agricultural commodities, both domestically and for export. "We know that in the first semester of 2021, from January to June 2021, exports from the agricultural sector will reach IDR 282 trillion or USD 1.95 billion. This figure is up 4.05 percent compared to the same period in 2020, which is Rp. 247 trillion or USD 1.71 billion," he said.

On that occasion, Jokowi mentioned porang, swallow's nest, and edamame as the mainstay of the agricultural sector for export. Moreover, the price of porang in the export market stimulates farmers to continue planting and cultivating these tubers.

"I saw in the field like I said that in the future, porang will make the export market which is still very large. But I entrust this porang commodity to be encouraged to produce finished goods, either in the form of cosmetics in the form of rice or other foods," said Jokowi.

In a dialogue with porang farmers in Madiun, Thursday (19/8), Jokowi found their enthusiasm in growing this export commodity. "Millennial farmers, young farmers in our village in Madiun, in the past, graduated from school and looked for work in the city. If not now, sir, graduated from school and became a porang farmer. Three years of struggling porang farming, after three years of bringing home a car," said Yoyok Triyono , one of the porang farmers told Jokowi, Thursday (19/8).
